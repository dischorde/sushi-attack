How To Play

Your goal is to dodge the flying sushi, earning points each time a sushi reaches the top of the screen untouched. Each level has a set amount of sushi to clear before moving on to a new kind of faster sushi.

Earn extra points by dodging special sushi like:
California Rolls
Tuna Rolls
Salmon Roe Rolls
Grab power ups such as:
Powerups that change your size
Powerups that change the sushi speed
Shields that keep you safe from sushi for a few seconds
Sushi Bombs that clear the screen of all sushi
Powerups grant extra lives
And powerups that give bonus points!!
