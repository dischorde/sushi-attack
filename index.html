How To Play

Your goal is to dodge the flying sushi, earning points each time a sushi reaches the top of the screen untouched. Each level has a set amount of sushi to clear before moving on to a new kind of faster sushi.

Earn extra points by dodging special sushi like:
  • California Rolls
  • Tuna Rolls
  • Salmon Roe Rolls
And grab power ups such as:
  • Increase/Decrease Size
  • Increase/Decrease Speed
  • Sushi Shields
  • Sushi Bombs
  • Extra Lives
  • Bonus Points
